What is this used for?

We utilize this program to compare players in different regions. It is a tool for those who want to create a national level teams or for managers who wish to trade or pick up new team members.

Who are we?

We are a group of players who like to see stats when comparing teams from different regions. We set up this website in 2014 while also managing our own respective teams.
